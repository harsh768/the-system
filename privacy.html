Privacy Policy — The System

Last updated: 2026

The System is a personal habit & goal-tracking app. We designed it to be private by default.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts and no servers of our own. Everything you enter — your quests, progress, notes, weight, and any budget figures — is stored only on your device (in the browser/app local storage). We do not see it, collect it, or transmit it.

Backups

If you use “Export Backup,” a file is created and saved by you, on your device. Importing a backup reads a file you choose. These never leave your device unless you share the file yourself.

Third parties

The web version loads a font from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of normal web requests. The app is hosted on GitHub Pages. We use no analytics, ads, or trackers.

Children

The app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page.
